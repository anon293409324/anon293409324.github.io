The EVM Jello PaperÂ¶

The Jello Paper is an attempt at defining the EVM semantics using the KEVM project. Unlike the Yellow Paper, the Jello Paper is an executable semantics, and can provide a full EVM interpreter usable for testing contracts, analyzing gas usage, verifying contracts correct, and a wide range of other tasks as specified in the [anonymized] technical report on KEVM.
